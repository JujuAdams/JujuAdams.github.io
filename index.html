Juju Adams is a London-based programmer and porting engineer, focusing on delivering high-end technical services to the GameMaker community.

He enjoys building games that push the engine into new territory, technically and narratively, specialising in optimisation and console development.

Outside of work, he dedicates his time to passing on skills to upcoming creators. Juju is a regular guest lecturer at universities across the UK, and writes guest blogs for GameMaker's parent company, YoYoGames. He runs the monthly Unofficial GameMaker Meetup, and a large Discord server for developers of all aptitudes.
